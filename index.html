About scroll.

scroll. is an interactive art piece that explores the nature of social media consumption and its impact on our emotional well-being.

As you scrolled through this feed, you probably noticed a gradual shift in content: posts that once felt engaging and meaningful gave way to ones that seemed increasingly empty and repetitive. This mirrors the experience many of us have on social media platforms, where the initial dopamine hits of interesting content give way to endless scrolling through increasingly hollow material.

The piece invites you to reflect on your own habits. How often do you find yourself scrolling without purpose? What emotions linger afterward? And beneath it allâ€”what are you really seeking?
